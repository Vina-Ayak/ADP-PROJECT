Home Remedy Recommender

Headache Remedies

Stay hydrated, rest in a dark and quiet room, or apply a cold compress to your forehead. Massaging your temples with peppermint or lavender oil can also help alleviate tension headaches.

Cold Remedies

Drink warm fluids like ginger tea or chicken soup. Inhaling steam infused with eucalyptus oil can relieve congestion. Gargle with warm salt water to soothe a sore throat.

Stomachache Remedies

Drink chamomile or peppermint tea to relax the stomach muscles. Ginger, either as tea or in small pieces, helps with digestion and reduces nausea.

Stress Remedies

Practice deep breathing or meditation to calm your mind. Drink herbal teas like chamomile or green tea. Physical activity, such as a short walk, can also help reduce stress levels.

Back Pain Remedies

Apply a heating pad or an ice pack to the affected area for 15-20 minutes. Gentle stretching exercises and improving your posture can help alleviate chronic back pain.

Allergy Remedies

Use a saline nasal spray to flush out allergens from your nasal passages. Local honey may help build immunity to pollen over time. Keep windows closed during high-pollen days.

Dry Skin Remedies

Apply coconut oil or aloe vera gel to moisturize the skin. Avoid hot showers, and use a humidifier if the air is dry.

Nausea Remedies

Drink ginger tea or suck on ginger candy to reduce nausea. Peppermint tea or sucking on a lemon wedge can also help alleviate the queasy feeling.

Acne Remedies

Apply tea tree oil or aloe vera gel to pimples. A honey and cinnamon mask can reduce inflammation and fight bacteria.

Fever Remedies

Stay hydrated with water and electrolyte-rich drinks. Use a cool damp cloth to sponge the body. Avoid heavy blankets to allow heat to escape.
